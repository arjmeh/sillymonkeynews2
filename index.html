Ukrussiarussia: A Faction

Image from Wikemedia Commons

A small conservative faction found in the suburbs of eastern
Ukraine had dubbed themselves, “Ukrussia.” Although
this newly formed faction has been disregarded by both
Nato and the USA respectively, Ukrussia has now allied with Russia,
forming a newly formed faction, dubbed, “Ukrussiarussia.”
This divergent faction has faced fire on Social Media, from
platforms like Twitter, Snapchat, and most famously, TikTok.
More trouble would come for Ukrussiarussia. Economic
downturn from both Ukraine and Russia has impacted
Ukrussiarussia more than the respective countries.
And even a more devastating loss, when Donald McDonald,
a supporting American billionaire, has gone
overweight and declined from society, thus making funds
for Ukrussiarussia, practically impossible to get. More reports

Apple is Closing Down After Lawsuit
With Microsoft

Image from Tech Pluto

Apple is closing down after a lawsuit with Microsoft.
Apple was given a copyright claim for copying one of Microsoft's
products: Microsoft Word. Apple developed Apple Word,
an almost identical replica of Microsoft Word. After a few
months, Microsoft employee Jen Stevenson found out that
Apple was copying Microsoft. She quickly filed a copyright
claim with the Washington Police and immediately caught
Apple's attention. Tim Cook tried to fight with the lawyer
but immediately realized that he wouldn't win. He was
devasted when the lawyer sent him to jail and forced Apple
to shut down on March 1, 2022. Many people try to justify
what Apple did but the lawyer did not get Tim a bail. Tim
Cook said that he would try to start a new company
called Orange, but he is in jail for the next seven years
so he can't. If you have an Apple product, the warranty
will expire, and the product will stop working in 21 days.

Ukrainians Continue to Battle For Kyiv

Image from The Guardian

Russian military forced their way into Kyiv, attacking by land and air.
Ukrainian soldiers came in to defend their capital. Volodymyr Zelenskyy
vowed he would not surrender until every Russian soldier was gone. Vladimir
Putin even called him a terrorist. On Friday, Mr. Zelenskyy posted a video
from Kyiv stating that "We are all here, defending our independence."
Ukrainian troops blew up bridges leading to Kyiv to slow the progress of
Russian tanks that were moving towards the city.

Image from INews

Ukrainian tanks and artillery were positioned to deflect a ground assault
that Russia was hinting at. Ukraine was able to withstand the first Russian
assault but was heavily impacted by the bombings. Ukrainian officials even sought
help from the U.S., and other Western countries, warning them of the
consequences that would ensue. In the White House, Jen Psaki warned
"Kyiv falling is a real possibility. Even as we see resistance on the ground, Russia's
military continues to advance toward Kyiv." Mr. Putin's attacks raised the risk that
violence could spread into other surrounding countries. If Moscow wins,
Russia could have the potential to place forces on the border of NATO. Oleksii
Reznikov stated that "Ukrainians are now fighting for the future they deserve.
For themselves and all of Europe." The Biden administration said that they
were thinking of offering support to the Ukrainian military, but defense
officials have not yet stated when this assistance would be delivered.
